Curmo Designs - Adult Education, Learning Material & (Rural) Community Development products

Research and Needs Assessment: 

CURMO offers expertise in educational and (economic/community) development research. A tested and flexible instrument is used for effective assessment of development status and needs. It provides for qualitative and quantitative dimensions and data interpretation. The organisation has done extensive research and model development on economic development issues.

 

 

Project Management: 

CURMO assists clients in the planning and management of projects. Experts provide advisory services on all aspects of project planning, implementation and monitoring.

 

 

Development of Learning Support Materials: 

CURMO directors have extensive experience in textbook writing and programme development. They offer expertise in development of learning support materials based on various learning approaches. Leaning programmes usually include Learner Modules, Portfolio Activities and Facilitator and Assessor Manuals.

 

 

Training: 

CURMO offers full qualifications e.g. the National Certificate in ABET Practice (NQF L4), National Higher Certificate in ABET Practice (NQF Level 5) and National Diploma in ABET Practice (NQF L5), as well as various NQF aligned skills courses. Course facilitation centers round written modules and includes group discussions, simulations, lectures and other appropriate learning and assessment activities.Assessment is conducted according to guidelines and criteria set by SAQA and requirements set by the ETQA in terms of OBE assessment approach and principles. Well-qualified course facilitators and registered assessors and moderators are involved in programmes.

 

 

Certified Skills Courses in the Lean Management System: 

Lean and HR experts facilitate arange of Lean Management courses to apply Toyota Business Practice. Courses include practical applications.

 

NQF Advisory Services, Learning Programme Design and Assessment Services: 

Curriculum experts offer services in the design of NQF aligned learning programmes, delivery strategies and assessment practice.

 

When needed, the organisation draws expertise and capacity from a network of associates. It has now also entered into a joint venture agreement with MKZ Business Development Services to form a Joint Business Academy, with head office in Puthaditjaba. The focus of the joint venture is entrepreneurial development, training in entrepreneurial and management skills and business support.
